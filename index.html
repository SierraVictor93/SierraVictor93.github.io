About

I am a first year Electrical Engineering student at the University of Waterloo, currently seeking employment for my work term during the Spring of 2016 (May 2016 to August 2016). I have a wide variety of interests including (but not limited to): Physics, electronics, web development, software testing, and quality assurance/control. I am a Singapore Citizen holding Canadian Permanent Residency, making me legally eligible to work across the country. I am also available for Co-op opportunities across the world. Prior to starting my studies at the University of Waterloo, I served in the Singapore Navy as a Command-and-Control system operator, from 2011 to 2013. My side interests include soccer, music, military history, and naval doctrine and technology.
